# SubSweep - Subscription & Free Trial Manager

SubSweep helps you track all your subscriptions and free trials in one privacy-first dashboard. No login required. No bank connection. Data stays in your browser.

## Quick Facts
- Privacy-first: all data in localStorage, zero server tracking
- Categories: Streaming, Productivity, Fitness, Cloud, News, Food, Gaming, Music, Other
- Tracks monthly/yearly/weekly billing cycles
- Free trial countdown with urgency alerts (3-day, 7-day warnings)
- "What else could this buy?" comparison tool
- Built by Neal Frazier as Day 2 of #30Days30Apps

## Tech
- React 19, Vite, Tailwind CSS v4
- Deployed on Netlify
- Static single-page application (SPA)
